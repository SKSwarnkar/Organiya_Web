About Organiya Global LLP

Premium Indian Superfoods • Global Export Capabilities • Trusted by Wellness Brands Worldwide

Our Story

Organiya Global LLP was founded with a simple mission: to bring India's rich heritage of natural nutrition to the world. Our journey began on family-owned farms where we have grown vegetables, fruits, and herbs using clean and sustainable methods for years. As global demand for plant-based and functional nutrition increased, we expanded our focus to export-grade Moringa, Turmeric, Ashwagandha, and premium superfoods trusted by health-conscious consumers and wellness brands around the world.
