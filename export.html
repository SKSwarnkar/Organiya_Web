Logistics & Shipping

Sea and Air Shipments

We offer both sea freight (economical for large volumes) and air freight (faster delivery) options. Shipping method selection depends on order quantity, urgency, and destination requirements.

Document-Ready Dispatch

All export documentation is prepared in advance and included with shipments. We ensure compliance with destination country regulations and provide complete paper trails for customs clearance.

Global Delivery Partners

We work with reliable international freight forwarders and shipping partners to ensure timely and secure delivery to your port or facility. Tracking information is provided throughout the journey.

Minimum Order Quantities

Minimum export orders typically start at 500kg per product. For larger quantities, we offer competitive pricing and dedicated support. Custom packaging and private label options available.
